EXPERT TIPS

How to Score 650+ in NEET

📚

NCERT is Bible

Read NCERT Biology line by line. 80% of NEET questions come directly from NCERT text and diagrams. Don't skip a single line.

⏱

Attempt Order Matters

Attempt Biology first (most marks, less time), then Chemistry, then Physics. Never start with Physics — it takes more thinking time.

📝

3 Mocks Per Week

Give at least 3 full-length mock tests per week. Analyse every single wrong answer. Don't move on without understanding why you got it wrong.

🔄

Revise Daily

Spend 30 minutes every morning revising previous day's topics. Spaced repetition is the most proven method for long-term retention.

🧬

Draw Diagrams

Draw all NCERT Biology diagrams at least 3 times. Diagrams are heavily tested in NEET — cell structure, heart, kidney, brain, etc.

❌

Manage Negative Marking

Skip questions you're less than 60% confident about. −1 per wrong answer adds up fast. Unattempted = 0, wrong = −1. Be strategic.
