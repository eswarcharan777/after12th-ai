🏢 About Us

Built for India's Students

We built After12th AI because we believe every Indian student — regardless of city, background, or budget — deserves a personal mentor for the most important exams of their life.

OUR MISSION

Making quality education accessible to every Indian student

Every year, 25 lakh+ students appear for NEET and JEE. Most of them spend the crucial 2-3 month gap after Class 12 boards with no clear direction — searching Google randomly, unable to afford ₹1–2 lakh coaching fees, and confused about which branch or college to choose.

After12th AI was built to change this. We combine the power of Claude AI (by Anthropic — one of the world's leading AI safety companies) with India-specific exam data to give every student a personal mentor, available 24/7, at a fraction of coaching cost.

Whether you're from a metro or a tier-3 city, whether you can afford coaching or not — After12th AI is here for you.
