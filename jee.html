STRATEGY

How to Score 250+ in JEE Main

■

Maths = Easy Marks

Calculus, Algebra, and Coordinate Geometry contribute 40% of Maths marks. Master these before anything else.

⚗

Chemistry First

Chemistry is most scoring. Start exam with Chemistry - it boosts confidence and leaves time for Physics numericals.

🔢

Integer Questions

Integer-type questions have NO negative marking in JEE Main. Never skip them - attempt all, even if you're not sure.

📅

2 Sessions Advantage

JEE Main has 2 sessions per year. Your best percentile counts. Use Session 1 as practice if unprepared.

⚡

Physics Numericals

Solve minimum 20 Physics numericals daily. Speed is crucial - you have only 2 minutes per question.

★

Qualifying for Advanced

To qualify for JEE Advanced, you need to be in top 2.5 lakh. Target 97+ percentile in JEE Main.
