How to Crack NEET 2026 — Complete Strategy Guide

Published July 5, 2026 · Updated for NEET 2026 syllabus

NEET is one of India's toughest entrance exams, with 25 lakh+ students competing for ~1 lakh MBBS seats. Here's the exact strategy that AIR toppers use to score 700+.

📊 NEET 2026 Exam Pattern

  • 200 minutes (3 hours 20 min)
  • 200 MCQs — attempt any 180 (45 each Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology)
  • +4 for correct, -1 for wrong
  • Total 720 marks

📈 Subject-wise Weightage

Biology (50% weightage — most important): Focus on Genetics, Human Physiology, Plant Physiology, Ecology.

Chemistry (25%): Physical Chem for numericals, Organic for reactions, Inorganic (NCERT is enough).

Physics (25% — trickiest): Mechanics, Electrodynamics, Modern Physics carry highest weight.

📅 6-Month Study Plan

Month 1-2: Complete NCERT Class 11 for all 3 subjects. Solve chapter-end questions.

Month 3-4: Complete NCERT Class 12. Start weekly mocks.

Month 5: Full-length mocks 2x/week + weak topic revision.

Month 6: Only PYQs (last 10 years) + revision. No new topics.

📚 Best Books for NEET 2026

  • Biology: NCERT (must!) + Trueman's Biology
  • Physics: HC Verma + DC Pandey
  • Chemistry: NCERT + MTG Objective NCERT

🎯 Topper Tips

  1. NCERT is not enough — it is everything. Read 3+ times.
  2. Take one full mock every week. Analyze mistakes for 2 hours after.
  3. Biology diagrams are gold — practice drawing them.
  4. Don't skip Physics thinking Bio will save you. Physics decides top ranks.
  5. Sleep 7-8 hours. Zero compromise on health.
