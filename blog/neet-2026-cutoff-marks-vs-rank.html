🎯 Category-wise Cutoffs (AIQ)

General: ~50th percentile — around 138–140 marks

OBC / SC / ST: ~40th percentile — around 108–110 marks

EWS & PwBD: Same as UR minus 5-percentile relaxation

Note: These are qualifying cutoffs. Admission cutoffs (esp. for MBBS/BDS in Govt colleges) are much higher — usually 600+ for AIIMS, 550+ for top state Govt colleges under 15% AIQ.

🏥 Score Needed for Popular Colleges

  • AIIMS Delhi: 705+ (rank < 50)
  • Maulana Azad Delhi: 680+ (rank < 200)
  • JIPMER Puducherry: 670+
  • Grant Medical Mumbai: 640+
  • State Govt MBBS (85% state quota): 500–600+ (varies by state)

💡 State Quota Advantage

85% of state Govt medical seats are reserved for state domicile students. If you're from Andhra, Karnataka or Maharashtra, your effective cutoff is often 30-40% lower under state quota vs 15% AIQ.
