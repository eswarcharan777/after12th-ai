Best Books for JEE Main 2026 — Topper-Approved List

Published July 22, 2026 · Curated from AIR <100 rankers

There are hundreds of JEE books on Amazon. You need 3-4 per subject, not 30. Here's the exact stack top rankers use, plus free alternatives if you can't afford paid books.

📐 Mathematics

  • NCERT Class 11 + 12 — Non-negotiable. Every formula/definition JEE tests starts here.
  • RD Sharma (Objective) — Foundation-building, huge problem set.
  • Cengage Maths Series (G. Tewani) — Best for advanced problem-solving. One book per topic (Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate, Trigonometry, Vector-3D).
  • Arihant Skills in Mathematics — Optional. Use for weak topics only.

🔬 Physics

  • NCERT Class 11 + 12 — Especially Modern Physics, EM Waves, Semiconductors (direct JEE Main questions).
  • HC Verma Vol 1 & 2 — Concept clarity + solved examples. Do every problem.
  • DC Pandey (Arihant) — JEE-oriented, topic-wise practice.
  • Irodov — Only if aiming for JEE Advanced top 500. Ignore for JEE Main-only.

⚗️ Chemistry

Physical Chemistry: NCERT + P. Bahadur (Numerical Chemistry) + N. Awasthi (Cengage)

Organic Chemistry: NCERT + Himanshu Pandey (Cengage) — best for mechanism problems. MS Chouhan for reaction practice.

Inorganic Chemistry: NCERT is enough. Read it 3 times. Optional: JD Lee (concise version) for periodic trends and coordination compounds.

🎯 Previous Year Questions (Most Important)

  • Arihant 44 Years JEE Chapter-wise — Solve after each chapter.
  • MTG JEE Main Chapterwise PYQ — More recent (2019 onwards, when JEE Main went online).
  • Free option: NTA official website has last 3 years' papers as PDF.

💸 Free / Low-Cost Alternatives

If budget is tight, this stack costs under ₹1,500 and covers 90% of JEE Main:

  • NCERTs (~₹500 total for Class 11+12 all subjects)
  • HC Verma (₹400 second-hand)
  • Free NPTEL/PW videos on YouTube
  • After12th AI mock tests + AI tutor (free)

📅 Study Order (When to Read What)

Months 1-3: NCERT + coaching material only. Build the base.

Months 4-6: Add HC Verma, Cengage Maths topic-by-topic.

Months 7-9: Full mocks + PYQ books + weak topic revision.

Last month: Only PYQ + revision notes. No new books.
