NEET vs JEE — Which Exam Should You Choose After 12th?

Published July 5, 2026 · Career guide

The big decision after Class 12: medical or engineering? Here's a complete breakdown to help you decide.

Quick Comparison Table

FactorNEET (Medical)JEE (Engineering)
SubjectsPhysics, Chem, BiologyPhysics, Chem, Maths
Study duration5.5 years (MBBS)4 years (BTech)
Avg salary₹8-15 LPA (after PG)₹8-25 LPA (fresher)
Job securityVery highHigh (tech booms)
DifficultyMemory + conceptsProblem-solving heavy

Choose NEET if you...

  • Love Biology and dislike heavy Maths
  • Want stable, respected career
  • Are OK with long study duration (5.5+ years)
  • Enjoy helping people directly

Choose JEE if you...

  • Love Math and problem-solving
  • Want faster earnings (start job at 22)
  • Interested in tech, AI, coding
  • Prefer variety of career options
